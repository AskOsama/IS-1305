Online Bank Security Scenario

Experience security from three different perspectives: Bank, Customer, and Attacker

🏦 Bank's Perspective

Protecting assets, maintaining trust, and ensuring compliance

🎯 Primary Goals

  • Protect customer funds and data
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Maintain regulatory compliance
  • Preserve customer trust
  • Ensure 24/7 service availability

🔒 Security Measures

  • Multi-factor authentication
  • End-to-end encryption (TLS)
  • Fraud detection algorithms
  • Transaction limits and alerts
  • Regular security audits

⚠️ Risk Concerns

  • Data breaches and leaks
  • Account takeover attacks
  • Insider threats
  • DDoS attacks on services
  • Regulatory penalties

👤 Customer's Perspective

Convenient access while keeping money and identity safe

🎯 Primary Goals

  • Keep money safe and secure
  • Easy access to accounts anytime
  • Protect personal information
  • Quick notification of suspicious activity
  • Simple and intuitive interface

🛡️ Customer Responsibilities

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Check for HTTPS in browser
  • Never share credentials
  • Report suspicious emails

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Using weak passwords
  • Falling for phishing emails
  • Banking on public WiFi
  • Ignoring security alerts
  • Sharing account access

🦹 Attacker's Perspective

Understanding threats helps build better defenses

🎯 Attack Goals

  • Steal money from accounts
  • Harvest login credentials
  • Commit identity theft
  • Sell stolen data on dark web
  • Disrupt banking services

⚔️ Common Attack Methods

  • Phishing emails and websites
  • Malware and keyloggers
  • Man-in-the-middle attacks
  • Social engineering calls
  • Session hijacking

🎪 What Stops Attackers

  • Strong encryption (breaks interception)
  • MFA (makes stolen passwords useless)
  • Fraud detection (catches anomalies)
  • Security awareness (users don't fall for tricks)
  • Regular monitoring (quick response)
